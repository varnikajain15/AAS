ABOUT AAS

" जहाँ आस है, वहाँ विश्वास है "

AIM

We aim to provide an open platform to all the citizens of our country on which they can vocalize and localize their work. Irrespective of the sector in which they belong. To make a webapp that will be for all types of people whether they are farmers, businessmen, exporters, importers, teachers, traders, or anyone else. To serve a user-friendly environment so that people can interact with each other and work smoothly and effectively. To serve some additional features through a web page such as a chat option so that buyers can connect with sellers directly, a platform where teachers can share their study material among students by just sharing a key, and many more.







OUTCOME

As said by Ronald Raegan

“We can’t help everyone but everyone can help someone.”

By this small initiative, we can help many people by just providing a platform. Amid COVID-19 many new challenges arise for all of us. We will connect people with people to help each other through this web app. We can be a part of VOCAL FOR LOCAL by supporting small businesses through this web app as it will help them to showcase their work. The people you support are the people who grow.
Every small step in the right direction can take our country one step forward and turn the things that look impossible today into tomorrow’s truth!
